Calculating Cement and Sand Needs in Plastering Walls Now, to calculate the cement and sand requirements in this plastering wall is not difficult. But even so, you still may not just as long in determining the composition or make a plastering mortar. This is because in the calculation process, you must still hold the safety standards for this work. Then how do you get the right composition? You only need to apply the right calculation formula using a simple multiplication system. Basically according to SNI 2008 there are three types of compositions in the ratio between cement and sand in using a mortar. The cement comparison here will be marked with a PC while the sand comparison uses the PP mark. For this calculation, we use a size of 1 sack with a weight of 40 kg. Well, this time we will discuss 3 types of sand and cement comparison compositions used in making mortar wall plastering as follows: 1. Plastering with 1 PC composition: 4 PP In SNI 2008, the making of a plastering mortar that uses a ratio of 1 PC: 4 PP with the amount of cement 6.24 kg / m2 and sand 0.024 m3 / m2. Now, from this calculation, you can find out the amount of cement and sand needed by multiplying the size of the area to be plastered with this calculation. So suppose you want to plaster a wall with a size of 15 x 6 m then the amount of cement needed is (15 × 6) x 6.24 = 90 x 6.24 = 561.6 kg or about 14.04 bags. Then for the sand needs are (15 × 6) x 0.024 = 90 x 0.024 = 2.16 m3. 2. Plastering with 1 PC composition: 5 PP In SNI 2008, the making of a mortar that uses a ratio of 1 PC: 5 PP with the amount of cement 5.18 kg / m2 and sand 0.026 m3 / m2. Now, from this calculation, you can find out the amount of cement and sand needed by multiplying the size of the area to be plastered with this calculation. So suppose you want to plaster a wall with a size of 15 x 6 m, the amount of cement needed is (15 × 6) x 5.18 = 90 x 5.18 = 466.2 kg or about 11,655 bags. Then for the sand needs are (15 × 6) x 0.026 = 90 x 0.026 = 2.34 m3. 3. Plastering with 1 PC composition: 6 PP In SNI 2008, the making of a plastering mortar that uses a ratio of 1 PC: 6 PP with the amount of cement 4.42 kg / m2 and sand 0.027 m3 / m2. Now, from this calculation, you can find out the amount of cement and sand needed by multiplying the size of the area to be plastered with this calculation. So suppose you want to plaster a wall with a size of 15 x 6 m, the amount of cement needed is (15 × 6) x 4.42 = 90 x 4.42 = 397.8 kg or about 9,945 bags. Then for the sand needs are (15 × 6) x 0.027 = 90 x 0.027 = 2.43 m3.(http://www.anekabangunan.com/menghitung-kebutuhan-semen-dan-pasir-pada-plesteran-dinding/#:~:text=Menghitung%20Kebutuhan%20Semen%20dan%20Pasir%20Pada%20Plesteran%20Dinding,-Nah%2C%20untuk%20menghitung&text=Jadi%20misalkan%20anda%20ingin%20memplester,90%20x%200.026%20%3D%202.34%20m3.)
